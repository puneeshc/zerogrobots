Problem: Traditional Robots Don't Work in Space

Wheeled rovers need gravity and a surface with friction

There is no gravity in space, which means no friction, which means wheels cannot work.

Walking / crawling robots need gravity and a surface

Newton's 3rd Law of motion doesn't allow for walking or crawling in space in the absence of something to push off of.

Flying drones need an atmosphere

Flying drones require an atmosphere, which is absent in the vacuum of space.
